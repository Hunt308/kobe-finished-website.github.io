About


My name is Kobe Rosenberg. I was born and raised in Miami; Florida, and currently reside in Saint Augustine; Florida. Throughout my life, I have been captivated by the power of visual communication and its ability to create meaningful connections. In 2013, I learned to master Adobe programs starting with Illustration and Photoshop before expanding upon to other programs to fully benefit both my skills and the clients too.


What can I do for you:


  • Branding and Identity Design: I specialize in developing cohesive brand identities that accurately reflect the essence of your business or organization. From logo design to brand guidelines, I create visually striking and memorable brand assets.
  • Print Design: Whether it's brochures, posters, or business cards, I craft engaging print materials that leave a lasting impression. I pay meticulous attention to detail and ensure that the final product conveys your message effectively..
  • Digital Design: In this digital age, I understand the importance of creating eye-catching digital assets. From website graphics to social media content, I create visually appealing designs optimized for online platforms.
  • Illustration: I possess a keen eye for detail and a passion for illustration. I can create custom illustrations that add a unique touch to your project, bringing ideas to life in a visually compelling way.
  • Collaborative Approach: I believe in the power of collaboration and strive to understand your vision, goals, and target audience. I work closely with my clients, valuing their input throughout the design process to ensure that the final product meets their expectations.
